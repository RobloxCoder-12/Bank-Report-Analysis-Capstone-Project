Key Highlights 

Data Engineering: Imported raw loan datasets into MS SQL Server, performed data cleaning, transformation, and created queries for KPIs (MTD, MoM trends).

Interactive Dashboards: Built three dashboards (Summary, Overview, Details) in Power BI & Tableau showing loan quality, regional distribution, purpose breakdown, and borrower demographics.

KPI Monitoring: Automated tracking of Good vs. Bad Loans, Total Funded Amount, Amount Received, Average Interest Rate, DTI.

Business Impact: Improved visibility into loan health and repayment trends, enabling faster risk assessment and better financial planning
